Tinywhoop-style FPV quad

  1. Home
  2. Projects
  3. Microquad

Specifications

  • FC + VTx: betafpv Lite Brushed FC V2
  • Motors: betafpv 7x16mm 19000KV
  • FPV Camera: Caddx Ant Micro
  • Rx: FrSky XM+

In detail

Assembled for indoor FPV fun, this tinywhoop-style microquad uses one of betafpv's excellent integrated flight controller/VTx boards, with a built in OSD that allows for setup without a PC.

It also works with my existing Taranis X-Lite Tx with the additiion of the FrSky XM+ Rx onto the board, and a little 'light' canopy modification to allow the antenna's to poke through the top.
