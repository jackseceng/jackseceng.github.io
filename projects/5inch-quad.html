Specifications

  • Frame: Martian II RX250
  • Flight Controller: Mamba F405 Mk2 Stack
  • Motors: Emax ECO Series 2207s
  • FPV Camera: Foxeer Falkor
  • HD Camera: GoPro Hero 5 session
  • VTx: Eachine TX805
  • Rx: FrSky XM+
  • GPS: Beitian BN-180
  • Buzzer: ViFly Finder 2

Build Update:

I've added a GPS module and a recovery buzzer to the frame. This allows for return-to-home functionality and the ability to find the quad even if the main LiPo disconnects. Thanks to the community in the uavfutures discord server.
